Design Projects

Luke Crane & Projects

The logo for Luke Crane and Projects is a modern design that represents precision, strength, and innovation. Featuring a blue crane, it symbolizes the company's focus on construction and large-scale projects. The crane's dynamic posture conveys movement and progress, while the blue color evokes trust and professionalism.

The Ultimate Music Lounge

The logo for The Ultimate Music Lounge features a gold guitar as the "G" in "Lounge" and "I" in music, symbolizing the venue's dedication to music and entertainment. The combination of elegant typography and the gold guitar element reflects a premium, vibrant atmosphere, appealing to music lovers.

Luvuno

The logo for Luvuno Nail and Beauty Bar combines elegance and nature, with a green leaf incorporated into the "V" of "Luvuno." This design element reflects the business's commitment to natural beauty and self-care, while the clean, modern typography adds sophistication.

Ziyanda Trading

The logo for Ziyanda Trading features a simple yet bold "ZT" motif, representing the company’s streamlined approach to business. The clean and minimal design reflects professionalism and efficiency, making it suitable for a wide range of industries.

Macc Gee

The logo for Macc Gee, a clothing brand by popular social media influencer @macc_gee (Lesedi McGlory Riba), uses a clean, straightforward text-based design. This minimalist approach highlights the brand's focus on style and authenticity.

Shriekplaas Farm

The logo for Shriekplaas Farm is a simple, timeless design that evokes the essence of farm life. With natural elements and earthy tones, the logo reflects the farm’s commitment to sustainability, agriculture, and community.
