DESIGN WORK

A collection of logo designs and branding projects

Luke Crane & Projects

A modern logo design representing precision, strength, and innovation. Featuring a blue crane, it symbolizes the company's focus on construction and large-scale projects. The crane's dynamic posture conveys movement and progress, while the blue color evokes trust and professionalism.

The Ultimate Music Lounge

Features a gold guitar as the "G" in "Lounge" and "I" in music, symbolizing the venue's dedication to music and entertainment. The combination of elegant typography and the gold guitar element reflects a premium, vibrant atmosphere, appealing to music lovers.

Luvuno Nail and Beauty Bar

Combines elegance and nature, with a green leaf incorporated into the "V" of "Luvuno." This design element reflects the business's commitment to natural beauty and self-care, while the clean, modern typography adds sophistication.

Ziyanda Trading

Features a simple yet bold "ZT" motif, representing the company's streamlined approach to business. The clean and minimal design reflects professionalism and efficiency, making it suitable for a wide range of industries.

Macc Gee

A clothing brand logo by popular social media influencer @macc_gee (Lesedi McGlory Riba), using a clean, straightforward text-based design. This minimalist approach highlights the brand's focus on style and authenticity.

Shriekplaas Farm

A simple, timeless design that evokes the essence of farm life. With natural elements and earthy tones, the logo reflects the farm's commitment to sustainability, agriculture, and community.
